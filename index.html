$ make dev

Create a dev environment in minutes!

Visually select the required services, configure your images, get a ready to use `docker-compose.yml`

Be the first to try it: 
      
        version: "3"
        redis:
          image: redis:alpine
          volumes:
            - ./docker/redis/:/data
        mariadb:
          image: mariadb
          volumes:
            - ./docker/mariadb/:/var/lib/mysql
          environment:
            MYSQL_RANDOM_PASSWORD: 'yes'
            MYSQL_USER: makedev
            MYSQL_PASSWORD: makedev
            MYSQL_DATABASE: makedev
        api:
          build: .
          ports:
            - '3131:80'
